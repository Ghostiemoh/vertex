## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary**: Web3-curious freelancers and contractors who value speed but find crypto friction intimidating.
- **Secondary**: Web3-native power users who demand high-performance settlement and professional infrastructure.
- **Job to be Done**: Move capital instantly with zero friction (turbulence) and high trust.

### Brand Personality
- **Voice**: Authoritative, frictionless, and rapid.
- **3-Word Personality**: Flow, Velocity, Precision.
- **Tone**: Engineering-led but human-accessible. High-speed fluid dynamics.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual Tone**: **Vertex Flow**. Smooth vector paths, glassmorphism used for physical depth (refraction), and telematic typography.
- **Anti-Reference**: "AI Slop." No generic glowing cards, no purple/blue gradients, no stock Unsplash imagery.
- **Theme**: Dark Mode (Engineering/Night Flight feel) with high-key Emerald accents.

### Design Principles
1. **Zero Turbulence**: Every interaction must feel like it's moving through a vacuum. No unnecessary clicks.
2. **Haptic Depth**: Use the "Double-Bezel" technique for all containers to simulate machined hardware.
3. **Telemetric Clarity**: Technical data (amounts, signatures, dates) must use high-precision Monospace for "truth."
4. **Cinematic Motion**: Every state change (loading, success) must be a micro-choreographed spring animation.
